All Hopper buses are fitted with free WiFi!

So, whether you want to tweet or update your Facebook status, check your emails, surf the web or crush some candy, simply connect when you get on the bus and surf away, saving your data allowance!

Connecting to WiFi is easy, simply:

    * Ensure WiFi is enabled on your phone or device

    * Select nct-buses

    * Accept the T&Cs and press Connect

    * Enjoy FREE WiFi on the bus
